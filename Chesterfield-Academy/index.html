The Chesterfield Academy is a warm and nurturing space where your child thrives each and every day.
Our school provides a fun learning environment for children ages infant to 7 years old. Our preschool offers STEAM focused curriculum taught by our outstanding faculty. A separate school building provides a private pre-kindergarten, Kindergarten & school age programs. We also provide before/after school and a school age summer camp. Call 314-111-0101 to schedule a personal tour.

Meet Jaya

Head of the school

Hello, my name is Jayshree, Jaya for short. and I am the Head of School for the Chesterfield Academy. I have a bachelor’s degree in elementary education from Maryville University. I strive for excellence and providing quality childcare, while promoting a fun and positive workplace for our staff. Prior to becoming Head of School, I worked as a director and classroom teacher for several years. My experience in these has been a valuable tool in understanding both the needs of our students and our employees. My goal is to build strong relationships and create a sense of community within our school. I enjoy spending time with my family . I love Singing and dancing and enjoy watching Bollywood movies.
